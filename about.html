Our Mission at Revenza

We design tools that make upselling natural, precise, and performance-driven so merchants can sell smarter, not harder.

2. Revenza Story

Every Great Product Begins with a Real Problem

Every good product has a story. Ours started with two colleagues and one brilliant idea.

We are Sarthak Jha (Developer) and Yatish Vijay (Support Expert), two friends who met at work, bonded over shared challenges, and realized we both wanted to build something meaningful for merchants.

Like many builders, we each tried separate paths first. We faced setbacks, learned the hard way, and kept moving. The turning point, however, did not come from a strategy deck or a business meeting.

It came from a casual evening conversation with Sarthak's wife, who said: "Why don't you guys build something together?"

That one sentence changed everything. No overplanning. No waiting for a perfect time. Just a decision to start that same night.

We combined technical execution and customer-first operations, then built our first product for Shopify merchants to:

  • Increase revenue with relevant add-ons
  • Improve conversion rates without disruption
  • Create better shopping experiences customers enjoy

What began as an idea over a drink became a focused mission: help merchants grow with smarter systems and better product experiences.
